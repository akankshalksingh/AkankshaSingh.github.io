Sales Dashboard for Business Management

  • Project URL: Sales Dashboard for Business Management

This project involves creating a Sales Dashboard using Tableau and a Kaggle dataset to provide a powerful visual tool for decision-making in the industry. The dashboard presents key metrics, such as total sales growth and distribution by state, revealing insights like the dominance of the 'Technology' category and highlighting top-performing managers. The objective is to contribute analytical skills to drive business growth and optimize processes through well-informed decision-making.
